# openkoutsi

**openkoutsi** is a self-hosted cycling coaching platform. Upload your rides,
track how your fitness is trending, and follow a training plan built around your
own goals — all from a server you control.

> **koutsi** (κουτσί) — Finnish for *coach*.

!!! note "These docs are a work in progress"
    This site is the user guide for openkoutsi. It explains how to *use* each
    feature. It is **not** a developer or API reference. Pages will be expanded
    over time — if something is missing, check back later.

## Who it is for

- **Athletes** who want to own their training data instead of handing it to a
  cloud service.
- **Coaches** who need to view their athletes' activities and plans.
- **Self-hosters** running openkoutsi on their own hardware.

## What you can do

- **Bring in your rides** — upload FIT files directly, or sync automatically
  from Strava and Wahoo. See [Activities & sync](features/activities.md).
- **Track your fitness** — watch CTL/ATL/TSB (fitness, fatigue, and form) trend
  over time. See [Fitness metrics](features/fitness-metrics.md).
- **Follow a plan** — generate a periodized training plan, build structured
  interval workouts, and push them to your head unit. See
  [Training plans & workouts](features/training-plans.md).
- **Get coaching feedback** — optional AI-generated analysis of your activities
  and daily training status.
